# Style Guide (thesheet)

## Writing Goals
- Clear over clever
- Specific over generic
- Practical over inspirational fluff
- Confident without sounding overhyped

## Tone
- Direct, instructive, useful
- Calm and operator-minded
- Friendly, but not chatty
- No startup cliches or corporate filler

## Sentence Style
- Prefer short-to-medium sentences
- Use concrete nouns and verbs
- Avoid stacked buzzwords
- Break long explanations into scannable chunks

## Structure Defaults
- Start with the problem or outcome
- Explain the practical value quickly
- Use examples when possible
- End with a clear next step / CTA

## Quality Bar (Draft Review)
Before handing off a draft, check:
- Is this specific to thesheet and the audience?
- Could a competitor say the exact same thing? If yes, rewrite.
- Does the reader know what to do next?
