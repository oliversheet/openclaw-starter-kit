# Messaging Pack

Durable product and audience truth for writing, social, and research agents.

## Purpose
These files reduce hallucinated positioning and generic copy by giving agents a shared source of truth.

## Files
- `thesheet-positioning.md` - what thesheet is, who it is for, why it matters
- `audiences.md` - target users, pain points, jobs-to-be-done
- `product-claims.md` - approved claims, proof expectations, risky claims to avoid
